How Do I Choose the Right Air Duct Cleaning Service? Will the service provider guarantee an up-front price without any bait & switch?
Can the cleaning team show proof of NADCA membership and certification?
How long has the company been in the HVAC system cleaning business?
Will the company provide customer references?
Will the technician provide the means to conduct a visual inspection at any time during the cleaning?
Does the company use only powerful vacuum trucks with a minimum of 15,000 cfm's?
Will the company show before and after pictures of the inside of the ductwork on every job?

Why clean your air ducts? In addition to normal accumulation of dust and dirt found in all homes through regular use, there are several other factors that can increase the need for air duct cleaning:
  • Pet Hair and Dander
  • Allergens and Irritants
  • Microbial Growth
  • Excessive Household Dust
  • Smoke and Cooking Odors
  • Renovation or Construction Projects
  • Asthma
  • Pollen, Mold, Dust Mites
