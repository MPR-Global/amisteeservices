Commercial Fire
If your commercial property has suffered a fire loss, do not waste any more time. Call Amistee Air Duct Cleaning. It does not matter what the size of the fire was that happened in your commercial building, the unexpected event is both scary and destructive. The damage created from a fire loss can affect many aspects of your facility, including the ventilation system. Different types of soot and smoke contamination can be caused based on the type of fire that occurred. The damage from a plastics fire leaves heavy black soot damage while the damage from a protein fire leaves a sticky residue. The restoration of the ductwork after a fire can be complicated and should only be done by a qualified and professional air duct cleaning service provider.

Amistee Air Duct Cleaning specializes in the restoration of a commercial building’s entire ventilation system after a fire has occurred.Amistee Air Duct Cleaning has proven to be a leader in the air duct cleaning industry. The owners of Amistee understand the importance of offering a duct cleaning service that is comprehensive and thorough so they have taken every step of the vent cleaning process into careful consideration. It begins by making sure that our technicians have a complete understanding of all the components of an HVAC system, especially if the duct cleaning is necessary because of fire damage.

Each of our team members are trained according to the National Air Duct Cleaners Association (NADCA), which ensures that they have the highest qualifications in the most innovative and efficient methods of air duct cleaning. Our team understands that the level of soot damage is dependent on the source of the fire. Another important factor that will need to be considered is whether or not the heating and cooling equipment was running during the time of the fire. If so, the soot will be distributed throughout the entire ventilation system. These and many other factors are taken into consideration to make sure that the duct system in your commercial building is professionally cleaned.

Our technicians will systematically clean every component of your facility's HVAC system using industry-specific tools to remove the soot, fire extinguisher dust and any other contaminants that have collected within the ductwork and the heating and cooling equipment. Once the ventilation system is thoroughly cleaned, we will also use an antimicrobial deodorizer to deodorize the ductwork and eliminate the odors caused by the fire. The product we use is registered with the Environmental Protection Agency, has the lowest possible toxicity rating and is safe to use within the ductwork. It is not a residual product so within 48 hours, the product will have completely eliminated all the particulates within the ventilation system. When you choose Amistee, you can be confident that the quality of work performed will meet and exceed your expectations.

Our team is prepared to answer any questions you may have and demonstrate the core values of Amistee, honesty and integrity. Do not waste any more time. Call Amistee today and let our highly-trained experts demonstrate our level of expertise and professionalism.
