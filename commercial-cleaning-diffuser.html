Commercial Duct Cleaning Southeastern Michigan
Looking for an expert to answer your questions about cleaning the ductwork in your commercial property?Amistee Air Duct Cleaning provides a clear understanding of the techniques and equipment used in the commercial duct cleaning process in order to help you properly maintain the ventilation system of your commercial facility. Amistee is a member of the National Air Duct Cleaners Association (NADCA) and our team members are trained according to this standard. NADCA is a non-profit organization that sets rigorous requirements for its members in order to provide a higher level of assurance to business and home owners. The combination of this training program as well as our on-site training facility guarantees our professional technicians a higher level of expertise. The owners of Amistee are dedicated to providing a thorough and comprehensive cleaning and this begins by properly preparing our technicians to handle any unique situation. Whether you are a large industrial facility or standard office building, the team at Amistee has both the technical knowledge and advanced equipment to professionally clean the ventilation system in your commercial building.

According to the National Institute of Health’s Office of Research Services (ORS), an appropriate time to have the air ducts cleaned in your commercial building is if you see “dust discharging from the supply diffusers.” The Environmental Protection Agency (EPA) and other indoor air specialists have agreed with this statement. Diffusers are a common component in heating, ventilation, and air-conditioning systems, or HVAC systems. They are typically used for the supply side of your system and are located on the air terminals, or returns. Any debris or build-up that is visible on the diffuser is a sign that there are contaminants accumulating within your ventilation system and are polluting your indoor air.

Our commercial duct cleaning removes anything that has the potential to become airborne from your ductwork and includes every aspect of your HVAC system, including the supply diffusers. Our air duct cleaning specialists will individually remove the diffusers, air-wash their components and guarantee they have removed any of the pollutants that have accumulated on the diffusers. The comprehensive commercial duct cleaning is provided by our team of air duct cleaning specialists that use the most powerful equipment in the industry. When you choose Amistee as your commercial duct cleaning service provider, you can be confident that the quality of work you receive will meet and exceed your expectations.
