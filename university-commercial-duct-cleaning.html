University Campus Municipal Duct Cleaning
Need an air duct cleaning professional in Southeastern Michigan that has experience cleaning the ductwork on a University campus? In today’s world of rising energy costs, making sure your heating, ventilation and air-conditioning (HVAC) system is properly inspected and maintained is the best way to keep those costs low. According to the National Air Duct Cleaners Association, “When an HVAC system is a source of contaminants introduced into occupied spaces, properly performed system cleaning services should take place to reduce or eliminate contaminant introduction.” These contaminants can include allergens, mold spores, odors, dead skin cells, dust, dirt and other debris that will filter into the indoor air quality of the buildings on any University campus. In order to ensure the health of those living on or visiting the campus, a preventative maintenance plan should be put in place where routine cleanings prevent the accumulation of such unwanted contaminants.

Build-up within the HVAC system reduces proper air flow and the functionality of the heating and cooling equipment causing higher energy costs and reduced longevity for the equipment. Amistee Air Duct Cleaning understands the importance of addressing these issues and is considered a leader in the commercial cleaning industry. Our air duct cleaning professionals provide commercial duct cleaning services for buildings of all sizes. Our qualified team will work with building blueprints, building engineers and facility managers to make sure that every component of the HVAC system is thoroughly cleaned. Amistee also provides accommodating schedules to help coordinate the duct cleaning with campus activities. Call one of our knowledgeable customer care agents and discover the high level of professionalism our team offers.

Much like your University, our team values education. Each of our technicians are trained according to the National Air Duct Cleaners Association, which provides comprehensive knowledge of the most innovative methods in the air duct cleaning industry. Amistee is a proud member of this institution, which has the highest authoritative understanding of proper duct cleaning techniques. Our technicians comprehend every component of your HVAC system and will clean those aspects with precision. We use the most powerful equipment in the industry to remove anything that has the potential to become airborne from your ductwork system. Our PTO driven vacuum trucks provide 16,000 to 17,000 cfm’s, which is the most powerful vacuum permitted without damaging or collapsing your duct system.

The vacuum collection equipment we use properly disposes of any contaminants that have accumulated within your campus buildings’ HVAC systems. Our technicians will clean each component of your University’s ventilation system using specialized air-scrubbing equipment and compressed air tools. We use stringent techniques to prevent the cross-contamination of zones and make sure that the HVAC ducts are kept at the appropriate pressure. Our team members take pride in offering a quality duct cleaning service that goes unmatched and we consider that in every aspect of your University’s HVAC system cleaning. We make sure the process of scheduling an air duct cleaning for your University is convenient and accommodating. Our friendly team members will be able to answer any questions you may have and coordinate an appropriate time to have the HVAC system on your campus cleaned by a team of knowledgeable and professional air duct cleaning specialists.
