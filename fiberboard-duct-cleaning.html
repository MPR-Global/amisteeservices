Fiberboard Duct Cleaning Michigan
Amistee provides air duct cleaning to different kinds of ductwork, including fiberglass duct board, or fiberboard.Within the walls of your home is a maze of ductwork that provides air to every area of your residence. The ductwork is connected to your heating and cooling equipment and distributes the temperature evenly throughout your home in order to keep you and your family comfortable. The ductwork can be made of several different kinds of material, the most common being galvanized steel. No matter what material your ventilation system consists of, the team at Amistee Air Duct Cleaning is prepared with a complete understanding of all the components of your heating, ventilation and air conditioning system, or HVAC system and the most advanced equipment in the air duct cleaning industry in order to provide a professional and comprehensive cleaning of the duct system in your home.

Fiberglass duct board, or fiberboard, is one material that could have been used to construct your HVAC system. The fiberboard is cut into 4x8 lengths of fiberglass, modified to fit the proper size, and then either rolled up or folded over to create a circular or square duct. The edges are then taped and it is ready to be installed. The advantages to fiberboard are it is less costly than steel ducts, it is easier to assemble and it insulates the ducts resulting in less heat gain or energy loss. The fiberglass material exposed on the interior of the ductwork requires professional cleaning performed by a qualified air duct cleaning specialist. Amistee has both the level of expertise and the proven reputation to properly clean the fiberglass duct board, flexible ducting or traditional steel ducting of your ventilation system.

Amistee takes every part of the cleaning process into consideration in order to provide a professional and comprehensive air duct cleaning that the community of Southeastern Michigan can trust. We are members of the National Air Duct Cleaners Association (NADCA) and train each of our team members according to their standards. We also provide an on-site, hands-on training facility so our technicians can learn practical knowledge and continue to advance with the most innovative methods and techniques in the air duct cleaning industry. Our team is trained to be professional experts and we work hard to make sure that our quality of work meets and exceeds your expectations. We equip our air duct cleaning specialists with state-of-the-art equipment so they have the proper tools to perform a thorough vent cleaning of your entire HVAC system.

From the powerful PTO driven vacuum truck to the specialized air-scrubbing tools, our equipment is both efficient and well-maintained so we can provide an honest and reliable air duct cleaning service. When the vent cleaning is complete, we will also provide photographs that were taken before and after the cleaning so you will know you made a good decision when you chose Amistee Air Duct Cleaning. Amistee takes pride in offering a duct cleaning that will ultimately improve the indoor air quality in your home and this leaves no room for shortcuts. Our team values a complete cleaning at an affordable cost. Our professional care agents are available any time to answer any questions you may have and find a convenient time to schedule the duct cleaning.
